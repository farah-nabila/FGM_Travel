ABOUT KUNDASANG, RANAU,SABAH

Ranau is located in the West Coast of the Malaysian state of Sabah and make up a majority the peasants and farmers of the tribe of Dusun community. Ranau city located at valley of Mount Kinabalu which has an area of about 3,608 sq. km. Viewed in terms of geographical position (Sabah Map), the Crocker and Mount Kinabalu alignments are between Ranau and West Coast districts, Kota Belud and Kota Merudu, while the areas with the east coast are Sungai Sugut and Sungai Labuk. Most of the settlements are located in villages scattered around the valley and hill slopes. Ranau is famous for its highland vegetables and aromatic local tea brand unlike any other. Population in Ranau district is about 95, 800 people.

Ranau comes from the word ‘Ranahon’ which bringing the meaning of the area paddy field. Kadazan-Dusun community in Ranau district cultivation of crops paddy or hill paddy considering shape and face of the hills. But for those who stay in the Ranau valley and they cultivate of crops paddy field. The coincidence of this area has developed as district administration center and Ranau's name has been taken as introduction to Ranau district.
