HILL


Sosodikan (Pyramid) Hill, Kundasang

This place located at Bukit Sosodikon, 89308 Kundasang, Sabah. The place is charged a small fee which is about RM3. This mountain is an attraction for anyone who loves extreme activities. This is because to reach the top, they have to climb about 20 minutes. Upon arrival at the top, you will feel a feeling that will relieve all the fatigue of climbing. From the top of this peak, on the north side there is a view from the Kinabalu area and on the south side there is a view of the pyramid-shaped hills.

Sabah Tea Garden

Sabah Tea Garden is a tourist area that must be visited because the unspeakable natural beauty will definitely eliminate all problems. There are various activities that can be done by tourists here such as accommodation, canopy walk, restaurants, tea shops and more. In addition, you can also visit tea shops that provide authentic products from around Borneo such as Tenom coffee, herbs, batik sarong, black pepper, asam boi and so on. This place is located in KM17, Jalan Ranau / Sandakan, Kg. Nalapak, 89309, Ranau. Most importantly, no entrance fee is charged! So, do not miss this opportunity when visiting Ranau, Sabah.

Crystal hill

Crystal Hill is one of the lodges on the hill. It is located in Kundasang Ranau, Sabah. Starting from RM480 per night, Crystal Hill is also complete with a hot tub and near the center there is also a night market that is open daily. Tourists and residents do not have to worry about going to town even sitting in an area far from there. From this accommodation center, you will be able to enjoy the beauty of nature as it is located on a hill and certainly not tired of looking while enjoying the weather and refreshing air away from the city. Not only that, you can also see the view of the sun rising and setting from here. This can take away all the fatigue from the real world.

Kinabalu Pine Resort

Kinabalu Pine Resort is one of the attractive tourist spots to enjoy the beauty of nature. Located in Kampung Kundasang 88990 Ranau, Kundasang. The resort is a cabin house built from Selangan Batu hardwood and surrounded by pine trees. This place is said to be interesting because from the balcony of this accommodation, you can see the view of Mount Kinabalu and the surrounding landscape is also interesting accompanied by refreshing air and weather. The price of this resorts starts from RM192 per night.

WATERFALL


Air Terjun Kipungit

Kipungit Waterfall is one of the natural beauties found in Ranau, Sabah. Tourists can enjoy the beauty of the clear waterfall while you can soak your feet while waiting for small fish to come to remove dead skin like in spas. Here, you can enjoy it for as long as you want. You can also do family activities such as picnics and bathing with the family. The entrance price charged by the management is also not too expensive which is around RM3 to enter the waterfall, apart from that you can also feel the canopy walk around the price of RM3 and take a picture of RM5.

Air Terjun Langganan

Langganan Waterfall is one of the nature found in Ranau, Sabah. This waterfall is the highest waterfall in Sabah. The time taken to reach this waterfall is about 2 hours and the distance is about 4 km. Seems far away, doesn't it? Don't worry, along the way, you will definitely be accompanied by the beauty of flora and fauna found there and you will definitely forget all the fatigue. Entrance fee is also charged for this tourist spot of RM3 to go to the waterfall, RM3 canopy walk and RM5 to take pictures as souvenirs.

Kolam Air Panas Poring

Poring Hot Spring is a hot spring pool that contains sulfur which is said to be good for health and can provide freshness to the body and relieve sluggish joints. It is located in the lowlands of Ranau, Sabah, Poring Hot Spring. This pool is suitable for climbers of Mount Kinabalu after a tiring climb. This hot spring was built during the Japanese occupation and remains so to this day. This hot spring is also one of the natur found in Ranau, Sabah because of the natural base of the pool. There are also 2 other pools that you can experience if you need privacy with the family. The hot spring is open daily from 7 am to 6 pm and the entrance ticket price is about RM3 for adults and RM1 for children.

Tagal Sungai Moroli, Kampung Luanti

This place is a place where providing a fish spa. It's located at Kampung Luanti, Ranau, Sabah. People who like to go to spa must come to this ‘Tagal’ Sungai Moroli to enjoy the fish spa. In the Local KadazanDusun dialect, ‘tagal’ means ‘no fishing’, and the main purpose of the ‘tagal’ is to preserve the environment as well as the ecosystem to benefit the future generation. ‘Ikan Pelian’ or Malaysian Mahseer comes from the family of Cyprinidae. These fishes are specifically trained by the villagers to perform the healing massage activities. The fish massage is deemed to have a curative effect on those who suffer from psoriasis and it is suitable for them to experience this because this fish will be nibbling off your dead skin.
