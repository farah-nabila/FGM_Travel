Tips for backpacking

Here, we providing some tips for backpackers so that can help them when travel, Hope it can help you in having a good backpacking:

1. Don’t pack too much stuff

When backpacking, we need to know what stuff we need to bring and don’t. It is important to avoid you from carrying not useful stuff and it can affect you trip.

2. Travel with a group to split cost

You can travel with a small group to reduce your cost. It can help you in manage your budget for visiting other place. Other than that, you can build a good relationship with others when you travel together.

3. Stay at hotel with free breakfast

You also can staff at a hotel that providing a free breakfast. It can help you in save your budget for a breakfast at the same time you can save time from find other place to breakfast.

4. Eat street food.

It is important things that you need to do when you had a backpacking. Eat street food is an enjoyment that you must do and it surely can make you not stop eating that food. The best part of street food is it is a cheapest food that you can get.

5. Pack a camera

Picture is an important thing you must keep for your memory in that place. Camera is important to take every beautiful moment and surely you will not miss the longing for the place you are traveling.
